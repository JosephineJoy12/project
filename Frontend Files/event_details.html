Festival Wave: Where Music Meets Magic!

Festival Wave is Australia's premier multi-genre music extravaganza, set to electrify Wollongong Plaza, NSW from August 16-18, 2025. This three-day celebration of sound and culture brings together the best of rock, pop, electronic, and hip-hop music, featuring a stellar lineup including Courtney Barnett, Tame Impala, The KID Laroi, Flume, and Hilltop Hoods.
